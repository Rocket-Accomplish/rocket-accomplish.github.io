Our Mission

We are a non-profit that connects K-9 students who can't afford private tutoring with honor-roll high school students who want to contribute the community. Several of our tutors, including us founders, have gone through the academic challenges and have overcome the struggles. We know that we can not only help you with your school work, but also share with you lots of tips for improving your study skills.

Volunteer

During the COVID pandemic time, we have learned that so many elementary and middle school students are so left behind with their school work. They can't succeed in high school if they don't have a solid foundation. Our mission is to connect K-8 students who can't afford private tutoring with Honor-roll High School students who want to contribute to the community and earn volunteer hours. This is a win-win for both sides.
