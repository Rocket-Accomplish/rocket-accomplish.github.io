Volunteer with us!

Why tutor with us?

1. Directly work with students who need your help.

At Rocket Academics, you'll get one on one time teaching motivated students. Many of our students are underprivileged, so you can help change their education for the better.

2. Active coach community

Interact with our other student coaches within our community. Learn tips from our seniors on your college applications & help your juniors through their high school experience!

3. Volunteer with a flexible schedule.

We pride ourselves on not only providing a comfortable experience for our students but for our coaches as well. We will try our best to accomidate a flexible schedule for both you and your student.
